Welcome

Hello, and welcome. This is my documentation site, which essentially functions as a wiki. The contents of this website are completely open-source here, where I encourage you to contribute anything and everything you feel is reasonable to include here. The content featured here is first and foremost written in English, as it is my native tongue. Any translations are welcome, but I will not be able to maintain them myself‚Äîothers will need to ensure their relevancy and correctness.
